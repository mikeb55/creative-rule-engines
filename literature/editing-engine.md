# ✂️ Editing & Revision Engine

Use this engine to refine any prose draft.

## 1. Pass 1 – Structure
- Identify beginning/middle/end.
- Check scene purpose.
- Remove repetition.

## 2. Pass 2 – Character
- Check desires, obstacles, stakes.
- Ensure consistent motivation.

## 3. Pass 3 – Language
- Eliminate filler words.
- Replace vague adjectives with specifics.
- Strengthen verbs.

## 4. Pass 4 – Pace & Rhythm
- Vary sentence lengths.
- Balance dialogue with action and introspection.

## 5. Pass 5 – Line-Level Polish
- Improve clarity.
- Ensure each paragraph has internal flow.
- Strengthen the final line/image.

Use for both short stories and novels.
