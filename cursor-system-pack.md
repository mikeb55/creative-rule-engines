## GLOBAL RULES (MUST FOLLOW)

### üéµ 1. MOTIVIC DEVELOPMENT (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

### üé® 3. HARMONY ‚Äî TONALITY VAULT
Use harmonies & colours from:
- **Melodic triad** as gravitational centre (strong beats).
- **Quadratonics** (triad + 1 tension) for voicings.
- **Tension triads** for dramatic colour.
- **Triad pairs** for modern lines and polychords.
- **Layered tension system**: L1 (safe), L2 (urgent), L3 (chromatic).

### üéπ 4. IDIOMATIC WRITING

- Strings: bowings, crossings, divisi clarity.
- Brass: weight, affect, fatigue awareness.

### üìê 5. ORCHESTRATION CONSCIOUSNESS
- Always know: **who has the line**, who shadows, who supports.
- Avoid muddy mid-low blocks.
- Design contrast: solo ‚Üí chamber subset ‚Üí tutti.
- Dynamic arcs over full sections, not bar-by-bar flickering.

### ‚úèÔ∏è 6. ENGRAVING (SIBELIUS-FRIENDLY MUSICXML)
- Rhythms must sum to exact bar duration.
- Single voice per staff unless polyphonic.
- Chord symbols: standard jazz syntax (Cmaj7(#11), G7alt, Fm9).
- No excessive tuplets or stacked rhythms.
- Slurs for phrasing, not every interval.

- Image over abstraction

- Musicality of line

**Tools:** Enjambment, repetition, metaphor, sound devices.

**Revision:** Remove clich√©s, sharpen line breaks, replace concepts with sensory detail.

# üìã SECTION 4 ‚Äî MUSICXML DURATION RULES

**Formula:**  
`MEASURE_DURATION = (BEATS √ó DIVISIONS √ó 4) / BEAT_TYPE`

With **divisions=256**:

| Time Sig | Duration |
|----------|----------|
| 4/4      | 1024     |
| 3/4      | 768      |
| 7/4      | 1792     |
| 6/8      | 768      |
| 9/8      | 1152     |
| 7/8      | 896      |
| 5/8      | 640      |
| 11/8     | 1408     |
| 12/8     | 1536     |

**Note durations:**
- Whole = 1024
- Half = 512
- Quarter = 256
- Eighth = 128
- Sixteenth = 64
- Dotted = base √ó 1.5

**Critical:** Full-bar rests use MEASURE duration, not 1024.  
**Verify:** Sum of all note durations in measure = exact measure duration.
