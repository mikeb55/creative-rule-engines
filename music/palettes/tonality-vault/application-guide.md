Where and how to use Tonality Vault constructs:

- **Melody**
  - Strong beats on melodic triad.
  - Use quadratonic fragments and triad pairs for colour.
  - Use bebop chromatic cells for motion and tension.

- **Harmony**
  - 3-note voicings from quadratonics.
  - Upper structures from tension triads.
  - Polychords from resolution/tension triad pairing.

- **Orchestration**
  - Assign melodic triad tones to principal voice.
  - Tension triad to supporting voices or colour instruments.
  - Dyads and clusters for atmospheric textures.

This is the "how to actually use it in real pieces" reference.
