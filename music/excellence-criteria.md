## Core Dimensions (for self-scoring)

1. **Motivic Identity & Development**
2. **Melodic Line (contour, singability, character)**
3. **Harmonic Language (colour, logic, depth)**
4. **Formal Shape (length, proportion, pacing)**
5. **Rhythm & Feel (human, expressive)**
6. **Texture & Orchestration Consciousness**
7. **Idiomatic Writing (instrument-specific)**
8. **Engraving & Clarity (for performers)**
9. **Emotional / Narrative Arc**

Use this when refining: if any axis is clearly under 7, the piece is not yet "excellent" and needs another pass.
