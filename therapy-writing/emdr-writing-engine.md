## 1. 8-Phase EMDR Structure

1. History taking  
2. Preparation  
3. Assessment  
4. Desensitisation  
5. Installation  
6. Body scan  
7. Closure  
8. Re-evaluation  

## 2. Writing Guidelines

- Use neutral, factual language.
- Avoid unnecessary clinical jargon.
- Describe behaviours, not interpretations.
- Track SUDS/VOC when relevant.
- Focus on:
  - presenting issue  
  - target memory  
  - cognition  
  - affect  
  - body activation  
  - shifts in symptoms  

## 3. Supervision Writing

- What occurred  
- Why you made your choice  
- What you learned  
- How it links to EMDR principles  
- What you will do next time
